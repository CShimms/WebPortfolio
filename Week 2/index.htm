About Ibrahim Lincoln-Mercury Dealer

Abraham Lincoln, like most Gen Z idiots, owned 12 fidget-spinners and only traveled by electric scooter. He always shouted "Where the hell are the commissioners?" for some reason.

Where most Zoomers are content to rest on their nihilistic, meme-sharing laurels, Lincoln aspired to more. He wanted to be a Gen X rock star who added rap for some odd reason. Unfortunately, he was already too late for that, so he'll have to build a time machine at some point so he will have traveled back in time to the 90s to start Linkin Park after the end of his successful NBA career. I think after 26 years, he'll travel back in time again to win the Civil War and get shot by John Wilkes Booth, which is a weird way to choose to die when you have a time machine, but I guess the play was that good.

Achievements

  • Invented the first vomit-colored paint.
  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Invented the first self-contained underwater breathing apparatus.
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award, which is weird, because I don't think India existed back then
  • He was actually incapable of feeling pain, which later inspired the hit song Numb by Linkin Park.
